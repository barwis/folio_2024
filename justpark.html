JustPark

Mar 2017 – May 2017
Brief

JustPark is a technology platform that matches drivers with parking spaces through its website and mobile application.

My role

Led the extensive redesign and redevelopment of both web and mobile applications from the ground up, focusing on improving user experience and overall functionality. This involved working closely with cross-functional teams, including designers, product managers, and backend developers, to ensure a cohesive and intuitive interface across all platforms.

Conducted thorough user research and testing to identify pain points and areas for improvement, implementing modern design principles and cutting-edge technologies to create a seamless and engaging user experience. The project also included optimizing the performance and responsiveness of the applications, ensuring they meet the highest standards of efficiency and reliability.

Isomorphic React

Employed isomorphic   React to develop robust, high-performance applications that deliver a uniform and seamless user experience across both web and mobile platforms. This approach involved leveraging the benefits of server-side rendering to improve load times and overall application performance, ensuring that users received a fast and responsive experience regardless of the device they were using.

Additionally, I focused on creating a cohesive codebase that could be shared between web and mobile environments, reducing development time and maintaining consistency in functionality and design. By integrating isomorphic React, I was able to achieve a significant improvement in the efficiency and reliability of the applications, providing users with a smooth and engaging interface across all platforms. This method also facilitated easier maintenance and scalability of the applications, allowing for quicker updates and feature enhancements.

UI/UX

Optimized application performance through code refactoring, efficient state management, and implementation of best practices in React development.
