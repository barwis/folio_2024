Travel 2.0

In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, TMG recognized the need to enhance its operations through a comprehensive digital transformation initiative for Telegraph Travel.
Our team spearheaded the project, aimed at modernizing the Travel section of Telegraph portal, optimizing operational efficiency, and improving customer engagement.

Travel Portal

Our team also managed Travel's internal CMS, which was crucial for handling travel products and services. This Travel Portal app integrated data from multiple third-party APIs, consolidating information about various travel products. It empowered editors to add or modify content, tailoring it specifically to meet the needs of Telegraph's customer base.

I took ownership of the Travel Portal upgrade project, overseeing all aspects from planning to implementation. This comprehensive upgrade significantly enhanced the functionality and user experience of the portal. As a result of these improvements, the editorial team's efficiency increased by 40%, allowing them to manage content more effectively and streamline their workflow.
